About Us

Iguguno Secondary School is a community based one located at Iguguno ward, Mkalama District council in Singida Region alongside Singida-Mwanza road. tha school started in 2000 as ordinary secondary school and in 2016 form five class was established. We are dedicated to providing quality education to our students. We strive to foster a supportive learning environment that encourages academic excellence and personal growth from form one to form six.Our academic excellence lies on NECTA final examination results. Our greatest attention is to produce quality and an all-round responsible student who will serve our nation responsibly and professionally. Besides, we are focused to extend our lead by scoring first, second and third divisions only for form four and first division only for form six in every final examination prepared by the National Examination Council of Tanzania. This focus is fueled by our slogan that says"ELIMU NI MWANGA"
